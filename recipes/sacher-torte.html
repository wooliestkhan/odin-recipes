Sacher Torte

Description

Sacher Torte is a classic Austrian chocolate cake, made with a rich chocolate sponge and filled with apricot jam, coated in dark chocolate glaze.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz semisweet chocolate
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup apricot jam
  • 2 oz semisweet chocolate, melted

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Butter and flour a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. Melt 8 oz of chocolate and 1 cup of butter in a heatproof bowl over a pan of simmering water. Remove from heat.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat egg yolks with sugar until light and lemon-colored.
  4. Mix in vanilla extract and the melted chocolate mixture.
  5. In another bowl, sift together flour, baking powder and salt.
  6. Gradually fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture.
  7. In a clean, dry bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  8. Gently fold egg whites into the batter.
  9. Pour batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  10. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  11. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then remove from pan and let cool completely.
  12. Heat apricot jam and brush over the top and sides of the cake.
  13. Pour melted 2 oz of chocolate over the top and let it drizzle down the sides of the cake. Serve.
